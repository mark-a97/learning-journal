How To Make Orange Juice

Equipment you will need...

  1. Oranges
  2. Sugar
  3. A knife
  4. A blender
  5. Some water
  6. A sieve

Tutorial

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    Instructions: Add sugar and peel the oranges.

  2. Step 2

    Instructions: Peel the oranges and split them into two parts.

  3. Step 3

    Instructions: Take one part and cut into the middle of the oranges then proceed to remove the seeds.

  4. Step 4

    Instructions: Place oranges into the blender, add a small amount of water then blend.

  5. Step 5

    Instructions: Use your sieve and pour the contents of the blender into a jug to filter out other parts of the orange.

  6. Step 6

    Instructions: Pour the contents of the jug into a glass and enjoy your freshly made orange juice!
